# Asencio Research Group

**Asencio Research Group** is a research and analysis consultancy specializing in **AI evaluation and annotation, intelligence analysis, and legal and policy review**. We deliver high-accuracy, structured analysis for organizations that require trustworthy, defensible written outputs.

## Services

- **AI Evaluation & Annotation**
  - Response ranking and preference data
  - Hallucination detection and accuracy checks
  - Safety, policy, and instruction-following reviews
  - Clear rationale writing and QA

- **Intelligence Analysis**
  - Structured analytic techniques
  - Pattern identification and hypothesis testing
  - Intelligence-style briefs and executive summaries

- **Legal & Policy Review**
  - Issue spotting and argument evaluation
  - Policy interpretation support
  - Quality assurance for legal/policy writing

- **Research Synthesis & Reporting**
  - Summaries and decision-support briefs
  - Consistency and clarity review

## Approach

Our work emphasizes:
- Accuracy and disciplined reasoning
- Repeatable evaluation frameworks and rubrics
- Clear documentation and defensible conclusions
- Professional communication and on-time delivery
